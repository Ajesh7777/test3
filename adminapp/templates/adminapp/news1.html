SNA UNIT LEVEL ARTS COMPETITIONS 2018-19

Unit level SNA arts competitions were conducted on 12.01.19. Ms. Neenu P A arts club secretary inaugurated the function along with Dr.Sibin Joy Vayalil, Principal. The day was wonderful celebrations of the arts and students embraced the opportunities to compete in their respective classes and shared their talents. The highlight of the day was personality contest. This was a wonderful opportunity for the students to exhibit their personality skills. The day ended with the announcement of results of the competitions to the applause of the students.
